Parkinson's Disease

Parkinson's disease is a progressive neurodegenerative disorder that affects movement. It develops gradually, often starting with a barely noticeable tremor in one hand. Over time, the disease progresses and can lead to stiffness or slowing of movement.

Symptoms and Diagnosis

Common symptoms of Parkinson's disease include tremors, stiffness, and difficulty with balance and coordination. These symptoms can vary from person to person. Diagnosing Parkinson's can be challenging, as there is no specific test. Doctors rely on medical history, a physical examination, and sometimes imaging tests to make a diagnosis.

Treatment and Management

While there is no cure for Parkinson's disease, there are medications and therapies that can help manage symptoms and improve quality of life. Medications aim to increase dopamine levels in the brain, helping to control movement. Physical therapy, speech therapy, and occupational therapy can also be beneficial in managing the disease. Lifestyle modifications such as regular exercise and a healthy diet can also play a role in managing symptoms.

Introduction

Welcome to our innovative project focused on the early detection of Parkinson's disease through gait analysis. Parkinson's disease is a progressive neurodegenerative disorder that affects millions of people worldwide. Early detection is crucial for effective management and improved quality of life for those affected. Our project aims to provide a user-friendly platform where individuals can upload a video of their walking pattern for analysis.

How It Works
  • Upload Your Video: Users can easily upload a short video of themselves walking. The video should capture their gait from the front and side angles for accurate analysis.
  • Advanced Algorithms: Our system utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze the uploaded video. These algorithms are trained to detect subtle changes in gait that are indicative of Parkinson's disease.
  • Instant Results: Within moments, users receive detailed insights into their walking pattern. The system provides a comprehensive report indicating whether there are signs of Parkinson's disease present.
Why Gait Analysis?
  • Non-Invasive: Gait analysis is a non-invasive and cost-effective method for detecting Parkinson's disease. It does not require any physical contact with the patient, making it ideal for remote assessments.
  • Early Detection: Changes in gait can be one of the earliest signs of Parkinson's disease, even before other noticeable symptoms develop. Detecting these changes early can lead to earlier interventions and better outcomes.
  • Objective Assessment: Gait analysis provides an objective assessment of movement patterns, removing subjectivity from the diagnosis process.
Features
  • User-Friendly Interface: Our platform is designed to be intuitive and easy to use for individuals of all ages.
  • Secure Data Handling: We prioritize user privacy and ensure that all uploaded videos are securely processed and stored.
  • Detailed Reports: Users receive detailed reports that explain the analysis findings in a clear and understandable manner.
  • Account Management: Create an account to access advanced features, save reports, and track progress over time.
